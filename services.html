Our Services

The Students Union offers a variety of services to support and empower students. Below is a breakdown of our key services:

Advocacy

We represent the student body on important issues such as academic policy, welfare, and rights. Our team is always here to listen and act on behalf of students.

Academic Support

We offer tutoring services, workshops, and study groups to help students excel academically. Our academic advisors can also help with course-related concerns.

Clubs and Societies

Join one of our many student-run clubs and societies to meet new people, develop skills, and have fun! Whether you’re into sports, music, or academics, there’s something for everyone.

External Service Providers

We also partner with external organizations to provide additional services such as:

  • Student Loans
  • Health Services (NHS)
  • Mental Health Support (Mind)
